3D Visualization Project

This project focuses on creating a 3D visualization of a satay packaging design using Blender. The goal was to transform a flat packaging concept into a realistic 3D model that can be used for product visualization, presentation, and marketing. This project is part of a lecturer’s research project, and the design work produced in this project has received Intellectual Property Rights (Copyright / HAKI).

The project demonstrates skills in 3D modeling, material creation, lighting setup, and realistic rendering to produce a professional product visualization.

Software & Tools

  • Blender (3D Modeling & Rendering)
  • Adobe Illustrator (Packaging Design)
  • Adobe Photoshop (Texture Preparation)

Project Goals

  • Create a realistic 3D model of satay packaging
  • Visualize product packaging in a 3D environment
  • Apply lighting and materials to achieve a realistic render
  • Produce high quality product visualization

3D Process

The project started by designing the packaging layout in Adobe Illustrator. The design was then imported into Blender as a texture and applied to the 3D packaging model. Materials, lighting, and camera angles were carefully adjusted to produce a realistic product render.
